FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What courses does VibrantMinds offer?▼
We offer Java Full Stack, Software Testing, React + Node.js, Python Django, Data Science, and Android Development courses. All courses include placement support.
Is there a free demo class available?▼
Yes! We offer a free demo class for all our courses. You can book it by filling the contact form above or calling us directly. No commitment required.
What is the placement process?▼
After course completion, we provide resume building, mock interviews, aptitude training, and connect you with 500+ hiring partners. You can attend unlimited placement drives until you get placed.
Can freshers with no coding background join?▼
Absolutely! Our courses are designed for freshers and career switchers. We start from the very basics and take you to an industry-ready level step by step.
How can companies partner with VibrantMinds?▼
Companies can register on our platform to access our pool of trained candidates, post jobs, conduct campus drives, and use our online assessment tools. Contact us for a partnership discussion.
Do you provide EMI or instalment options?▼
Yes, we offer flexible payment options including EMI and instalments. Please contact our counsellors for details about the payment plan that suits you best.
